Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At PMERIT, we believe that education should be accessible to all while respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information as you use our educational platform.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, and password
  • Profile information (age, location, educational background)
  • Career assessment results and preferences

Learning Data

  • Course progress and completion status
  • Quiz and assessment scores
  • Interaction with Gabriel AI tutor
  • Learning preferences and patterns

Technical Information

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and location data
  • Usage analytics and performance data

How We Use Your Information

  • Personalized Learning: Create customized learning paths and recommendations
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your educational journey and achievements
  • AI Tutoring: Enable Gabriel AI to provide relevant, helpful assistance
  • Communication: Send important updates about courses and your account
  • Platform Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services
  • Security: Protect against fraud and ensure platform safety

Information Sharing & Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • Education Partners: With approved institutions for certification verification
  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who help operate our platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our users
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregate, non-identifying data for research and improvement

Data Security & Protection

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with 99.9% uptime
  • Limited access controls for staff
  • Blockchain verification for certificates

Your Rights & Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your learning data
  • Opt-out: Control marketing and non-essential communications

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Protecting learners under 13

Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

PMERIT complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and takes special care to protect the privacy of users under 13 years old.

Parental Consent

  • Accounts for children under 13 require verifiable parental consent
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information
  • Parents receive notifications about their child's progress
  • Educational oversight features available for parents and teachers

Limited Data Collection for Children

For users under 13, we collect only:

  • Basic account information (name, age-appropriate username)
  • Parent/guardian contact information
  • Educational progress and assessment data
  • Communications necessary for the educational service

Enhanced Safety Measures

  • No public profiles or usernames for children
  • Limited communication features
  • Content filtered for age appropriateness
  • No targeted advertising to children
  • Additional security controls and monitoring
