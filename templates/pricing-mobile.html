Frequently Asked Questions

Is PMERIT really free?

Yes! All core educational content, basic AI tutoring, community access, and your first attempt at every course is completely free. Optional premium features are available for those who want to accelerate their learning.

What happens if I can't afford the retry fees?

We offer compassionate exemptions for technical failures, documented hardships, or when you complete remedial activities. Our goal is to help you succeed, not create financial barriers.

How do optional modules help other students?

100% of profits from optional modules fund free education for underserved learners worldwide. By upgrading, you're directly supporting someone else's educational journey.

Can I cancel optional modules anytime?

Absolutely! All optional modules can be cancelled at any time with no penalties. Your core free access remains unchanged.
