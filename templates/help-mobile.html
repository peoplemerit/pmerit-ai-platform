Getting Started

To create your PMERIT account:

  1. Click "Sign Up" on any page
  2. Fill in your basic information (name, email, password)
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile setup
  5. Take the initial career assessment

Your account will be ready to use immediately after verification.

The career assessment helps us understand your:

  • Learning preferences and style
  • Current skills and experience level
  • Career interests and goals
  • Available time for learning

This information allows Gabriel AI to create a personalized learning path that matches your needs and goals.

PMERIT offers different access levels:

  • Free Access: Basic courses, AI chat, and community features
  • Premium AI Tutoring: $2.99/month for advanced AI features
  • Career Center: $4.99/month for job placement support
  • Certifications: Pay-per-certificate for verified credentials

Many features are completely free to ensure accessibility for all learners.

Courses & Learning

Course enrollment is simple:

  1. Browse the course catalog or get recommendations from Gabriel AI
  2. Click on any course that interests you
  3. Review the course description and requirements
  4. Click "Enroll Now" to add it to your learning path

Most courses are available immediately after enrollment.

Certifications

To earn a PMERIT certificate:

  1. Complete all required course modules
  2. Pass the final assessment (usually 70% or higher)
  3. Submit any required projects or assignments
  4. Pay the certification fee (if applicable)

Certificates are blockchain-verified and recognized by employers globally.
