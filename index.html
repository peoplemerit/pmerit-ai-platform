Getting Started

Begin your PMERIT journey by following these simple steps to set up your account and discover your personalized learning path.

Step 1: Create Your Account

Click the "Sign In" button in the top navigation and select "Create Account". Provide your basic information to get started.

Step 2: Take the Assessment

Complete our comprehensive personality and skills assessment to receive personalized course recommendations tailored to your learning style and career goals.

üí° Tip
Be honest in your assessment responses. The more accurate your answers, the better our AI can personalize your learning experience.

Step 3: Explore Your Dashboard

Your personalized dashboard shows your progress, recommended courses, and upcoming deadlines. It's your central hub for managing your learning journey.
