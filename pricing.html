Pay-for-Accountability Model

We believe accountability promotes success. Our compassionate approach encourages completion while supporting those who struggle. Assessment retries have small fees to encourage preparation, but we provide support at every step.

1

First Attempt: FREE

Your first attempt at every assessment is completely free. Take your time to prepare and demonstrate your knowledge without any cost.

2

Second Attempt: FREE + Tutoring

Need to retry? Your second attempt is also free, and we provide additional AI tutoring and study resources to help you succeed.

3

Third Attempt: ₦200 / $0.50

A small fee of ₦200 (approximately $0.50 USD) applies to encourage serious preparation. Hardship exemptions are always available.

4

Subsequent Attempts: Graduated Fees

Additional attempts have gradually increasing fees, but Premium subscribers never pay retry fees. Technical failures and exceptional circumstances always receive exemptions.

Note: Premium subscribers ($2.99/month) never pay retry fees. We also provide compassionate exemptions for technical failures, documented hardships, or completion of remedial learning activities.

How We Sustain Free Education

Transparency matters. Here's how PMERIT funds free education for billions of learners worldwide without selling your data.

Donations & Pay It Forward

Pay-it-forward contributions (1-5% of first-year salary increase), founding supporter donations, cryptocurrency donations, and individual giving from those who believe education breaks poverty cycles.

Corporate Sponsorship

Village sponsors, state sponsors, and national partnership programs. Companies committed to workforce development and corporate social responsibility fund scholarships and community education initiatives.

Enterprise Services

Custom training programs and white-label education solutions for organizations. Enterprise revenues directly subsidize free learner access, creating a sustainable model that serves everyone.

Premium Certifications

Optional verified certificates and professional credentials for learners who need formal recognition for employment or advancement. Learning is free; official certification is optional.
