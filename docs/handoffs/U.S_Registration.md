## PART 1: PMERIT HOLDINGS LLC (Parent Entity)

### Current Status
- **Entity Type:** Maine L3C (transitioning to standard LLC)
- **State:** Maine
- **Purpose:** Hold and manage subsidiary entities

### Transition Steps

1. **Amend L3C to LLC**
   - File Certificate of Amendment with Maine Secretary of State
   - Remove L3C designation requirements
   - Filing fee: $50

2. **Update Operating Agreement**
   - Add subsidiary management provisions
   - Include mission protection clauses
   - Specify distribution policies

### Operating Agreement Key Provisions

The Holdings LLC Operating Agreement should include:

1. **Purpose:** To hold and manage subsidiary entities for educational mission
2. **Subsidiaries:** Authority to create, manage, dissolve subsidiary entities
3. **Mission Statement:** Educational access for underserved communities
4. **Distributions:** Policy for profit allocation between entities
5. **Dissolution:** Assets to Foundation if Holdings dissolves

## PART 2: PMERIT FOUNDATION (501(c)(3) Nonprofit)

### Formation Steps

#### Step 1: Draft Articles of Incorporation

**Articles of Incorporation for PMERIT Foundation**

**Article 1: Name**
The name of the corporation is PMERIT Foundation.

**Article 2: Purpose**
The corporation is organized exclusively for charitable and educational purposes within the meaning of Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, including but not limited to:
- Providing free educational content and skill-building programs
- Empowering individuals with practical knowledge to improve their lives
- Supporting community-driven educational initiatives
- Delivering technology-enabled learning solutions to underserved communities

**Article 3: Registered Agent**
Name: [Insert Registered Agent's Name]
Address: [Insert Maine Address]

**Article 4: Incorporator(s)**
Name: [Insert Incorporator's Name]
Address: [Insert Address]

**Article 5: Board of Directors**
The corporation shall be governed by a Board of Directors of no fewer than three (3) members as outlined in the bylaws.

**Article 6: Non-Profit Provisions**
1. No part of the net earnings shall inure to the benefit of any private individual.
2. No substantial part of activities shall be attempting to influence legislation.
3. The corporation shall not participate in any political campaign.
4. Upon dissolution, assets shall be distributed exclusively to organizations exempt under Section 501(c)(3).

**Article 7: Signatures**
Incorporator: __________________________ Date: _______________

---

#### Step 2: File with Maine Secretary of State

- Submit Articles of Incorporation
- Filing fee: $40 (nonprofit corporation)
- File online: https://www.maine.gov/sos/cec/corp/
- Or file by mail with two signed copies

---

#### Step 3: Obtain EIN

- Apply online at IRS.gov (free, immediate)
- Required before opening bank account
- Required before filing Form 1023-EZ

---

#### Step 4: Draft Bylaws

**PMERIT Foundation Bylaws must include:**

1. **Board Structure**
   - Minimum 3 directors
   - Terms of service (recommend 2-3 years)
   - Officer positions (President, Secretary, Treasurer)

2. **Meetings**
   - Quarterly board meetings
   - Annual membership meeting
   - Quorum requirements (majority)
   - Voting procedures

3. **Conflict of Interest Policy** (Required for 501(c)(3))
   - Disclosure requirements
   - Recusal procedures
   - Annual certification

4. **Compensation Policy**
   - Reasonable compensation standards
   - Board approval for executive pay

5. **Dissolution Clause** (Required for 501(c)(3))
   - Assets to other 501(c)(3) organizations only

---

#### Step 5: Apply for 501(c)(3) Status

**Form 1023-EZ** (Recommended for PMERIT Foundation)
- Eligibility: Gross receipts ≤$50,000 for 3 years; Assets ≤$250,000
- Filing fee: $275
- Processing time: 2-4 weeks typically
- File online only

**Form 1023** (Full Application)
- Required if not eligible for 1023-EZ
- Filing fee: $600
- Processing time: 3-6 months
- More detailed application

**Key 501(c)(3) Requirements:**
1. Organized exclusively for exempt purposes
2. No private benefit or inurement
3. No substantial lobbying
4. No political campaign activity
5. Annual filing (Form 990-N or 990-EZ)

## PART 3: PMERIT TECHNOLOGIES LLC (For-Profit)

### Formation Steps

#### Step 1: File Articles of Organization

**Articles of Organization for PMERIT Technologies LLC**

**Article 1: Name**
PMERIT Technologies LLC

**Article 2: Registered Agent**
Name: [Insert Registered Agent's Name]
Address: [Insert Maine Address]

**Article 3: Organizer**
PMERIT Holdings LLC
[Insert Holdings LLC Address]

**Article 4: Management**
The LLC shall be managed by its member(s).

**Article 5: Purpose**
To develop, maintain, and operate technology platforms for educational purposes; to provide premium services; and to engage in any lawful business activity.

- Filing fee: $175
- File with Maine Secretary of State

---

#### Step 2: Create Operating Agreement

**Key Provisions:**
1. **Sole Member:** PMERIT Holdings LLC
2. **Management:** Member-managed
3. **Capital Contributions:** Initial contribution from Holdings
4. **Distributions:** At discretion of Holdings
5. **IP Ownership:** All platform intellectual property
6. **Tax Elections:** Default pass-through (or elect S-Corp if beneficial)

---

#### Step 3: Obtain EIN

- Apply online at IRS.gov (free, immediate)
- Separate EIN from Holdings and Foundation

---

#### Step 4: Register for Business Purposes

1. **SAM.gov Registration**
   - Required for federal contracts
   - Required for SBIR/STTR applications
   - Free registration

2. **Maine Revenue Services**
   - Sales tax registration (if applicable)
   - Withholding registration (if employees)

3. **Business Licenses**
   - Check local municipality requirements
