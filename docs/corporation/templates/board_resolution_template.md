## COMMON RESOLUTION TYPES

### Opening Bank Account
```
RESOLVED, that the Corporation is authorized to open a business
checking account at [BANK NAME] and that [NAME], [TITLE], is
authorized to sign checks and manage said account on behalf of
the Corporation.
```

### Accepting Grant
```
RESOLVED, that the Corporation accepts the grant award from
[GRANTOR NAME] in the amount of $[AMOUNT] for the purpose of
[PURPOSE], and authorizes the [TITLE] to execute all necessary
documents.
```

### Approving Contract
```
RESOLVED, that the Corporation enters into a [TYPE] agreement
with [PARTY NAME] for [DESCRIPTION] in an amount not to exceed
$[AMOUNT], and authorizes the [TITLE] to execute said agreement.
```

### Hiring Staff
```
RESOLVED, that the Corporation approves the hiring of [NAME]
for the position of [TITLE] at an annual salary of $[AMOUNT],
effective [DATE].
```

### Approving Budget
```
RESOLVED, that the annual budget for fiscal year [YEAR] in the
amount of $[AMOUNT] is hereby approved as presented.
```
