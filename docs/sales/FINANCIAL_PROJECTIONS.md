## 1. BUSINESS MODEL OVERVIEW

### Model Type: Freemium SaaS with Mission-Driven Core

PMERIT operates on a **sustainable freemium model** designed to maximize global educational access while generating revenue through premium services and corporate partnerships.

### Core Philosophy

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    "FREE FOREVER" PROMISE                       │
│                                                                 │
│   Core education remains free for all users, always.            │
│   Premium features enhance, never gate, the learning experience.│
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Revenue Model Summary

| Stream | % of Revenue (Y3) | Growth Driver |
|--------|-------------------|---------------|
| Premium Subscriptions | 18% | Individual upgrades |
| Corporate Partnerships | 27% | B2B enterprise deals |
| Donations & Grants | 45% | Foundation/individual giving |
| Certifications | 9% | Credential verification |

## 3. PRICING STRATEGY

### 3.1 Free Tier Value Proposition

**What's Always Free:**
- All 42 courses across 14 pathways
- Basic AI tutoring (Llama 3.1)
- 3 TTS voices
- Progress tracking
- Community access
- Mobile access
- Offline mode
- First assessment attempt
- Completion certificates

**Rationale:** Remove all barriers to education. Free tier must be genuinely useful, not a teaser.

### 3.2 Premium Tier Value Proposition

**Premium Justification:**
- Advanced AI = faster learning = faster career transition
- Premium TTS = better retention through superior audio
- No retry fees = unlimited practice
- Verified certificates = employer trust
- Job matching = tangible career outcomes

**Price Psychology:**
- $2.99/month = less than a coffee
- $29.99/year = less than a textbook
- Positioned as "investment in future" not "subscription"

### 3.3 Pay-for-Accountability Model

**Unique Revenue Mechanism:**

| Attempt | Fee | Rationale |
|---------|-----|-----------|
| 1st | FREE | Everyone deserves first chance |
| 2nd | FREE + Tutoring | Platform provides extra help |
| 3rd | ₦200 / $0.50 | Small accountability nudge |
| 4th+ | Graduated | Serious about learning |

**Exemptions:**
- Hardship applications accepted
- Premium subscribers exempt
- First-time learners exempt

**Projected Revenue:** $5K-$20K/year (not a primary stream)

## 6. UNIT ECONOMICS

### 6.1 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

| Channel | Cost | Conversion | CAC |
|---------|------|------------|-----|
| Organic/SEO | $0 | 2% | $0 |
| Social Media | $0.10/click | 1% | $10 |
| Referral | $5 reward | 5% | $5 |
| Paid Ads | $1.00/click | 0.5% | $200 |
| Partnerships | Revenue share | 10% | $0 |

**Blended CAC Target:** $2-5 per user

### 6.2 Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Free User LTV:**
- Direct revenue: $0
- Referral value: $2 (0.2 referrals × $10 value)
- Data value: $1
- **Total: $3**

**Premium User LTV:**
- Subscription: $72 (24 months × $3)
- Certifications: $50 (average)
- Referrals: $10 (1 referral)
- **Total: $132**

**Blended LTV:** $3 × 95% + $132 × 5% = **$9.45**

### 6.3 LTV:CAC Ratio

| Scenario | CAC | LTV | Ratio |
|----------|-----|-----|-------|
| Organic only | $0 | $9.45 | ∞ |
| Blended | $3 | $9.45 | 3.15:1 |
| Paid only | $10 | $9.45 | 0.95:1 |

**Target:** Maintain 80%+ organic acquisition for healthy ratios.
