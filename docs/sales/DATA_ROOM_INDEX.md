## CONTACT & PROCESS

### Inquiry Process

1. **Initial Contact**
   - Sign mutual NDA
   - Receive Level 2 access

2. **Preliminary Review**
   - Review documentation
   - Schedule demo call
   - Ask clarifying questions

3. **Deep Dive**
   - Request Level 3 access
   - Technical due diligence
   - Financial verification

4. **Negotiation**
   - Submit LOI
   - Negotiate terms
   - Final due diligence

5. **Closing**
   - Execute purchase agreement
   - Complete asset transfer
   - Begin transition support

### Timeline (Typical)

| Phase | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Initial Review | 1-2 weeks |
| Due Diligence | 2-4 weeks |
| Negotiation | 1-2 weeks |
| Closing | 1-2 weeks |
| Transition | 90 days |
| **Total** | **8-14 weeks** |
